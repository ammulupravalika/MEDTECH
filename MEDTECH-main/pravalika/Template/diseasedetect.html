ABOUTAccurate disease prediction needs expert knowledge as it is not understandable by common people.As there are infinite number of diseases ,they have different symptoms and combination of different symptoms lead to different diseases. So,it becomes a very complex issue to decide on a disease. Overall , this project solves the problem for people unaware of medical condition and also helps people of various economic classes to get a first opinion.It aims to assess whether it is possible to predict disease using machine learning.
